Hello! Welcome to the Georgianamart.com website. This website, including all related services, is operated by us – Navon. Our company is based in Romania. You can reach us at contact@navonjobs.com.

So, let's talk about these cookies. No, not the chocolate chip kind. These are digital cookies and similar technologies, like tags, web beacons, pixels and, for mobile apps, software development kits or SDKs, that help make your experience on the Website even better and to recommend content that might be of interest to you. When we talk about cookies in this policy, we are talking about all of these technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to make your browsing experience more efficient. We use various types of cookies and similar technologies, such as web beacons and pixels, to provide, secure, and improve our services. These technologies help us understand how you interact with our website and ensure a seamless experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

When you visit our Website and consent to cookies, these are the types of cookies that may be downloaded to your device:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website's basic functionality and needed for the administration and operation of the Website and app.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences to provide a personalized experience, showing you things you might be interested in.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us analyze website usage for improvements, counting visitors, checking how long they stay, and how they move around the Website.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and tracking across websites, remembering your visit to the Website, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed to other websites.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You’re in control of your cookies. You can adjust your preferences through the cookie banner on our website or through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our site.

Until you make up your mind, we'll only use the strictly necessary cookies.

Want to tweak your cookie settings? No worries. If you’re not keen on cookies or how we use them, it’s your choice -you can change the settings by adjusting the options on the banner that appears on the Website or through your browser settings.

For more detailed guidance on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org or allaboutcookies.org.

Just keep in mind, if you decide to go cookie-free (including the strictly necessary ones), you may not be able to use all the features on our Website.
